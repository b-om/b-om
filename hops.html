HOPS



What is HOPS ?

  • HOPS stands for Hospitality Operations.
  • It is web-based application which is built in latest technology.
  • It is used to keep track of your Restaurant and Board Game Cafe operations.
  • It helps you to run your Restaurant and Board Game Cafe smoothly and efficiently.

Why Use HOPS?

  • Improve Efficiency - HOPS allows your staff to perform revenue generating tasks such as taking orders, bill generation accurately which improves customer satisfaction.
  • Maintain Control - You can view the operations on mobile from any part of the world.
  • Digital Operations - HOPS responds faster as per consumer demands because of Digitized Operations.
  • Timely and Accurate Reports - HOPS allows you to analyze sales data. You can measure the effectiveness of pricing in minimum time.
  • Flexible Expansion Options - HOPS provides you the ability to add registers and administration computers.
  • Economical - You get the best deals as we charge for subscription monthly.

Key Features

  • Online Inventory Management.
  • Food Supply Management.
  • Food Order Management.
  • Table Management.
  • Board Game Management.
  • Digitized KOT
  • Comprehensive reporting.
  • Print Bills/Receipts.
